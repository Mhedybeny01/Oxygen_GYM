Customer experience

For each of the clubs in the network, Punch benefits from exceptional surfaces, thus allowing it to offer its members more than 10 training areas, hundreds of machines and sports equipment designed to adapt to the needs of each. And because each of you deserves to be encouraged to surpass yourself and achieve your sporting ambitions, Punch provides you with a team of certified and experienced professionals to support you during your sports training. Their mission is to make each Puncheuse or Puncheur a sportingly accomplished member.

An environment

Punch was not designed as yet another gym in Morocco, but as a real place of well-being for our members. This is why each Punch club has a friendly lounge offering a healthy menu as well as a range of elaborate food supplements. Stores are also available to customers for the acquisition of articles dedicated to sports practices as well as food supplements to the best international standards.
